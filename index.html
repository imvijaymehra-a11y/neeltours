Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our taxi services

How do I book a taxi with Neel Tour Travels?

Booking with us is easy! You can either call us directly at 9896037769/9996437769, send a WhatsApp message, or fill out the booking form on our website. Our team will confirm your booking within minutes.

What are your payment options?

We offer flexible payment options including cash, UPI, bank transfers, and digital wallets. For corporate clients, we provide monthly billing facilities with detailed invoices.

Are your drivers experienced and verified?

Absolutely! All our drivers undergo thorough background verification, professional training, and have valid commercial driving licenses. They have minimum 5 years of driving experience and are trained in customer service.

Do you provide 24/7 service?

Yes, we operate 24/7 for your convenience. Whether you need an early morning airport transfer or a late-night pickup, we're always available to serve you.

What types of vehicles do you have?

We have a diverse fleet including sedans (Swift Dzire, Etios), SUVs (Innova, Ertiga), luxury cars, and tempo travellers (12-26 seaters). All vehicles are well-maintained, air-conditioned, and regularly sanitized.

How is the fare calculated?

Our fares are calculated based on distance, vehicle type, and time of travel. We offer transparent pricing with no hidden charges. You'll receive a detailed fare breakdown before confirming your booking.
